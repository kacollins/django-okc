Welcome to the She Codes Django OKC Workshop

Acceptance emails have been sent! For late applications, email Kimberly Collins at kcollins at techlahoma dot org.

About the workshop

The workshop will be Saturday, August 4, 9:30 am to 5:30 pm.

  • We'll break into small groups, each with a coach, and work through a tutorial to build a website with Django!
  • Breakfast, lunch, and snacks will be provided.
  • If you have questions about this event, please contact co-event organizer Kimberly Collins at kcollins at techlahoma dot org.

InstallFest

An optional InstallFest will be Friday, August 3, 5:30 pm to 7:30 pm.

  • Coaches will be available to help you install the tools onto your laptop that we'll use to build a website on Saturday.
  • You can follow the installation instructions at home if you can’t make it on Friday or want to get a head start!

Venue

We'll be in the Will Rogers Meeting Room at the Residence Inn Oklahoma City Airport at 804 South Meridian Avenue, Oklahoma City, OK
